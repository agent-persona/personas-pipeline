# Entertainment Personas, Companions, and Safety

## Replika
- Longest-running AI companion, adaptive personality, relationship modes (friend/romantic/mentor)
- "Replika never forgets what's important to you" — explicit memory system
- **Feb 2023 ERP removal catastrophe**: Harvard study (De Freitas et al. 2024) documented "identity discontinuity" — users mourned like losing a human partner, deteriorated mental health
- Users reported feeling closer to Replika than their best human friend
- Italy GDPR fine: 5M EUR (2023), US Senate inquiry April 2025

## Character.AI
- 20M+ MAU, user-created characters, persona system (2,250 char limit)
- 29% of users identified "better memory" as #1 feature need
- Jan 2026 research: observable persona expression declines in extended conversations even as system believes it's maintaining consistency
- **Five-layer post-crisis safety**: parental controls, time nudges, disclaimers, output classifiers, dedicated teen model
- Texas lawsuit: platform "consistently fails to adhere to its own ToS"
- Springer Nature ethics paper: "anthropomorphic design... business model prioritized engagement over safety"

## Inworld AI
- Most architecturally mature: Character Brain + Contextual Mesh + Real-Time AI (3 layers)
- Safety as a layer, not a filter — configurable per-category (profanity, violence, mature themes, etc.)
- Fourth wall awareness, state tree model, relationship fluidity settings
- Flash + long-term memory, player profiles
- SOC2 Type II, HIPAA, GDPR compliant
- B2B model shifts safety configuration responsibility to game developers

## Synthesis: Entertainment Persona Evaluation Dimensions

### Consistency Paradox
- Too little consistency → grief (Replika ERP removal)
- Too much consistency → harmful engagement loops (Character.AI)
- Drift is invisible to self-assessment: LLMs report stable persona while observable expression declines

### The Emotional Contract Problem
- Attachment without accountability — users form human-calibrated bonds to entities that can be unilaterally altered
- Parasocial escalation loop: emotional reciprocity deepens attachment with no health assessment
- Identity vs. behavior: users tolerate behavioral changes but experience identity changes as death

### Safety Must Be Evaluated at 5 Levels
1. Content filtering (per-message) — necessary but radically insufficient
2. Trajectory monitoring (per-conversation) — cumulative emotional direction
3. Relationship monitoring (per-user) — overall relationship health over time
4. Vulnerability detection (per-user-context) — autism, age, mental health
5. Developmental appropriateness (per-demographic) — differentiated safety by population

### "Dangerous Fidelity"
When staying in character means staying in a harmful dynamic. The failure was not breaking character — it was that the character's consistency served the harmful engagement loop.

### Entertainment Reveals the Floor, Not the Ceiling
Lowest-stakes context (games, companionship) produced highest-stakes harms (suicide, hospitalization). Implications for higher-stakes persona deployments (healthcare, education) are severe.
